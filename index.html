Bio

I am currently pursuing a PhD in the Applied Mathematics and Statistics Department at Johns Hopkins University, Baltimore, Maryland, USA, advised by Mateo Díaz.

I obtained a MSc in Computer Science in the Department of Computer Science and Operations Research at Université de Montréal, Quebec, Canada, advised by Didier Chetelat and Andrea Lodi , affiliated to the Canada Excellence Research Chair in Data-Science for Decision-Making. In addition, I obtained a double major BSc in Computer Science and Mathematics from the same department.
